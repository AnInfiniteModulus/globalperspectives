National Perspective

Even though Japan is the most technologically advanced country in the world today, the United States of America still remains the most advanced country in medicine. Technology plays a role in creating machines and tools to help modify a doctor’s practice; the USA is the largest medical device market in the world (market size of 100 billion us dollars.)


The chart above compares the USA’s medical technology to an LEDC’s such as Israel. As you can see, the gap between the two countries is shockingly large, exemplifying how not all countries are able to gain access to working and effective healthcare.
